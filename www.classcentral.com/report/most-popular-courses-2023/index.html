The 100 Most Popular Free Online Courses (2023 Edition)

Using वर्ग केंद्र data and enrollments numbers to find the 100 most popular online courses of the year.

Suparn Patra Dec 6th, 2022
  • फेसबुक
  • ट्विटर
  • Envelope
  • 0

More than a decade has passed since a few स्टैनफोर्ड professors अपने पाठ्यक्रम की पेशकश करने का फैसला किया online for free. These later became known as MOOCs, for भारी मात्रा में खुले ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम, and 2012 became the “MOOC का वर्ष.”

Since then, the online course offering has continued to grow, to the point that it can be hard to navigate at times. In this article, I’ve leveraged वर्ग केंद्र‘s database of over 100,000 courses, as well as course enrollment numbers, to build a list of the 100 most-popular online courses of the year.

Methodology

I built this list following a well-defined methodology:

First, I went through the वर्ग केंद्र database and made a list of all the courses that were offered for the first time ever in 2022. This represented around 2600 courses.

Then, I wrote scrapers to collect enrollment numbers across four major online course platforms: Coursera, edx, फ्यूचरलियर, and स्वयं. These platforms show how many students are enrolled in each of their courses.

Finally, I sorted the courses according to their enrollment number and truncated the list to keep only the 100 most-popular courses.

Combined, the platforms considered accrued over 4M enrollments for courses launched in 2022. The 100 most-popular courses account for about half of them.

Interestingly, about 25% of the courses were created by गूगल, and about 15% by Meta and by आईबीएम. Tech companies are well-represented in this year’s list.
