When the pandemic hit more than 2 years ago, माइक्रोसॉफ्ट made लिंक्डइन लर्निंग पर 14 सीखने के रास्ते available for free. Since then, I’ve been keeping an eye on लिंक्डइन लर्निंग, regularly checking if there were more free लिंक्डइन लर्निंग certificates available. I searched through their catalog of close to 12,000 पाठ्यक्रम and 740 सीखने के रास्ते and identified the courses available for free and that offer free certificates.

I found 137 courses and 7 सीखने के रास्ते offering free certificates, as well as 378 free courses, for a total of about 1000 hours of free online learning.

We’re not sure how long most of these courses will offer free certificates. That said, we’ll continue to monitor लिंक्डइन लर्निंग and update the list regularly. In fact, the list initially had 350 hours of free resources. Now, it’s 1000 hours, so stay tuned!

How to earn a लिंक्डइन लर्निंग Free Certification

To earn a certificate for an individual course, watch all the videos and pass the quizzes. In some courses, you’ll need to take an exam. Once you’re done, go to the “Related to this course” section below the video (on desktop) and click on “Show all”.

A window will pop up with certificates available for the course, as you can see above. Click on “Download” to get your लिंक्डइन लर्निंग certificate. Some courses also offer Continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits, in addition to the लिंक्डइन लर्निंग certificate.

To earn a certificate for a learning path, you need to complete all the path’s courses. Then, you’ll be able to download the learning path certificate.
